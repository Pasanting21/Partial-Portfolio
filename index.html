Task Tracker

Tic Tac Toe

Tic-tac-toe, also known as noughts and crosses or Xs and Os, is a classic two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. The objective is to be the first to align three of your symbols (Xs or Os) in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Players take turns marking the grid, aiming to win by getting three of their symbols in a row before the opponent does.
